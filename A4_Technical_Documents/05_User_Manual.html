2. Registration

Public — No Login Required

2.1 Choosing Your Role

  1. Click Get started on the Landing Page (top-right button).
  2. The Role Selection screen appears. Choose:
    • Creator — influencer, content creator, social media personality
    • Brand — business, company, or marketing team
  3. Click your role card to continue to the sign-up form.
Figure 2.1 — Role Selection (/signup) Two cards — Creator and Brand — allow the user to select their account type before registration.

2.2 Creator Registration

Figure 2.2 — Creator Sign-Up (/signup/creator) Form fields: Display Name, Username, Email, Password, and optional social platform links.
Figure 2.3 — Brand Sign-Up (/signup/brand) Form fields: Company Name, Contact Name, Industry (dropdown), Email, Password.
  1. Fill in all required fields (marked with *).
  2. Password must be at least 8 characters with a number and a symbol.
  3. Click Create Account.
  4. Check your email inbox for a verification email from CreConnect.
  5. Click the link in the email to verify your address.
  6. Your account is now Pending Admin Approval. You will receive a second email once approved.
Note: Admin approval is required before you can log in. Approval typically happens within 24 hours. Contact creconnect2@gmail.com if delayed.

3. Login & Password Recovery

Public — No Login Required

3.1 Logging In

Figure 3.1 — Login Page (/login) Select your role (Creator / Brand toggle), enter email and password, and click Sign in. Google OAuth is also available. A server status indicator shows if the backend is reachable.
  1. Navigate to /login or click Log in in the navigation bar.
  2. Select your role using the Creator / Brand toggle.
  3. Enter your Email Address and Password.
  4. Click Sign in.
  5. You are redirected to your role-specific dashboard.

3.2 Forgot Password

Figure 3.2 — Forgot Password (/forgot-password) Enter your registered email. A 6-digit OTP is sent to your inbox. Enter the OTP, then set a new password.
  1. On the Login page, click Forgot password?
  2. Enter your registered email address and click Send OTP.
  3. Check your email for a 6-digit One-Time Password (valid for a limited time).
  4. Enter the OTP on the verification screen.
  5. Type and confirm your new password, then click Reset Password.
  6. Log in with your new password.
OTP expired? Click Resend OTP on the verification screen to receive a fresh code.

Figure 4.2 — Campaigns (/brand/campaigns) Lists all campaigns with status badges (Draft / Published / Closed). The + New Campaign button opens the campaign creation form.

6. Real-Time Messaging

CreConnect uses Socket.io for live, bidirectional messaging between brands and creators. Messages appear on both sides instantly — no page refresh is needed.

Brand Messages view
Creator Messages view

Starting a Conversation

As a Brand: Go to a collaboration or creator profile → click Message Creator. A thread opens in the Messages section.

As a Creator: Go to Messages in the sidebar. Threads with unread messages show a notification indicator.

Sending Messages

  1. Type your message in the text field at the bottom of the chat window.
  2. Press Enter or click the send button.
  3. The message appears instantly on both sides.
Note: If you go offline, you will receive an email notification for any messages sent while you were away.

8. Admin Panel

Admin Role Required
Restricted Access: The Admin panel is only available to accounts with the ADMIN role. Contact your system administrator for access.

8.1 Approving New Users

  1. Log in with an ADMIN account and navigate to Admin Dashboard.
  2. Click User Management in the sidebar.
  3. Filter by status PENDING to see new registrations.
  4. Click a user's name to review their details.
  5. Click Approve — the user receives an email and can now log in.
  6. Or click Reject — optionally enter a reason.

8.2 Running the AI Recommender

  1. Go to Admin Dashboard.
  2. Click Run AI Engine (or navigate to the AI Matching section).
  3. The system computes match scores for all creator–brand pairs using the hybrid engine.
  4. Results are stored in the ai_matches table.
  5. Brands will now see AI-ranked creators on their Discover page with match percentages.

8.3 Viewing & Acting on Reports

  1. Click Reports in the admin sidebar.
  2. View user-submitted reports (inappropriate content, spam).
  3. Take action: Dismiss, Warn, or Suspend / Ban the reported user.

9. Troubleshooting

ProblemLikely CauseSolution
Cannot log in — wrong credentials Incorrect email/password or unverified email Use Forgot Password to reset; check inbox for verification email
Account says "Pending" Admin has not yet approved the account Wait for approval email; contact creconnect2@gmail.com if delayed beyond 24 hours
Messages not appearing in real time Network connection dropped / WebSocket disconnected Refresh the page; check your internet connection
Campaign not visible to creators Campaign status is DRAFT Go to Campaigns → Edit → change Status to PUBLISHED
OTP expired before I entered it OTP has a time limit Click Resend OTP on the verification screen
Profile photo upload fails File too large or wrong format Use JPG or PNG under 5 MB
Apply button is greyed out Already applied to this campaign Check Collaborations → Pending for your existing application
Cannot see search results in Discover Creators No creators registered yet / filters too narrow Clear all filters and try a broader search
Server offline banner on login page Backend server is not running Use the demo accounts shown on the login page (Brand@123, Creator@123) to explore the app
